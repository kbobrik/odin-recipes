Super pancakes

Description:

Glorious golden pancakes. Ideal for a lazy Sunday morning. Will definitely make your day!

Homer Simpson approved!

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Maple syrup
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Bring the pan to medium heat and add some oil
  2. Mix eggs, milk and butter. Add a pinch of salt
  3. Pour the mixture on the hot pan and wait until the pancake turns golden
  4. Serve with the maple syrup
  5. Yum!
